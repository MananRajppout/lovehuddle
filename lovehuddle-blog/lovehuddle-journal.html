Why We Burned the Swipe.

👤

Kevin

Founder, LoveHuddle

Swipe culture wasn't an accident. It was engineered — deliberately — to maximise time-on-app, not to help you find a real connection. Every feature was designed to keep you scrolling, not to get you off the platform.

"The best dating app is one you delete. We're actually building for that."
